Delete Account – PromoFact

Last updated: 23 June 2026

You can request deletion of your PromoFact account and all associated data at any time by following the steps below.

How to delete your account

Option 1 – via email (recommended)
Send an email to toolsh.online@gmail.com with the subject line "Account deletion request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.
Option 2 – in-app (coming soon)
Account deletion directly from the app settings will be available in a future update.

What data will be deleted

  • Your account credentials (email / Google Sign-In link)
  • Your scan history and all scan results
  • Your feedback and any photos submitted via feedback
  • Your usage counters (free scans, purchased scan packages)

What data may be retained

  • Anonymised, aggregated statistics (no personally identifiable information) may be retained for service improvement purposes.
  • If you made in-app purchases, transaction records may be retained as required by applicable law (typically 5 years).

Processing time

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days. You will receive an email confirmation once your account has been deleted.

For any questions, contact us at toolsh.online@gmail.com.
